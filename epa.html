DOT & Federal Standards

Transportation of asbestos is strictly regulated by the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) under Title 49, Section 173.1090. We ensure full compliance with requirements for:

  • Detailed Shipping Documentation
  • Specialized Packaging & Labeling
  • Strict Vehicle Placarding
  • Minimized Occupational Exposure Protocols
