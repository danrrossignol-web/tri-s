About TRI-S Environmental Services

Privately Owned & Operated Since 1999.

For over 25 years, TRI-S Environmental Services, Inc. has been a leader in high-stakes environmental management across New England. We are not simply waste haulers; we are compliance partners dedicated to protecting our clients from liability through rigorous safety standards and expert execution.

Our team specializes in complex industrial projects, from large-scale site remediations to delicate lab-pack services.
