Containerized Waste Solutions

TRI-S provides cost-effective and reliable transportation and disposal services for a wide variety of regulated or hazardous drummed waste, totes, and cubic yard boxes (CBYs). We manage waste in liquid, solid, or semi-solid (sludge) forms.

Standard Container Options:

  • 55-Gallon Drums: The industrial standard for liquid and solid waste.
  • Custom Capacities: Smaller and larger capacity drums are readily available.
  • Bulk Totes & CBYs: Ideal for high-volume non-bulk waste streams.

*All containers are sealed in strict accordance with Federal EPA and State D.O.T. regulations.

Flexible Logistics & Hauling

Whether you have a single drum or a warehouse full of regulated materials, our fleet is equipped to handle the logistics safely and efficiently.

  • Full Truckloads (FTL): 48' and 53' trailers equipped with lift gates for easy loading at facilities without docks.
  • Less Than Truckloads (LTL): Dedicated 16' box trucks for smaller, agile pickups.
  • Compliance: Fully licensed hazardous waste transporters serving CT, MA, NY, and RI.

Professional Lab-Pack Services

The disposal of small-quantity, diverse chemicals requires high technical expertise. TRI-S Environmental provides a turnkey lab-pack service encompassing inventory, segregation, packaging, and transporting.

Chemicals Managed Include:

  • Acids, Bases, and Reagents
  • Aerosols & Compressed Gases
  • Cleaning & Disinfecting Agents
  • Inks, Dyes, Paints, and Thinners
  • Varnish Strippers
  • Oxidizers & Organic Peroxides
  • Toxic, Flammable, and Corrosive Materials
  • Explosive Materials & Unknowns
