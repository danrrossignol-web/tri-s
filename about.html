Our Mission & Capability

Founded in 1999 and headquartered in Ellington, Connecticut, TRI-S Environmental Services, Inc. is a premier full-service environmental firm. We provide turnkey solutions for complex environmental challenges, ranging from routine waste transportation to large-scale site remediation.

As a trusted contractor for the State of Connecticut for Oil & Chemical response, we operate with the highest level of accountability. Our firm manages hundreds of diverse projects annually, with the technical and financial capacity to execute single-contract projects valued up to $4,000,000.

Whether it’s a high-stakes emergency spill or a scheduled industrial facility cleaning, TRI-S delivers safety-first field services backed by decades of tenure.
