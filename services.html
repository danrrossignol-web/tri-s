Hazardous & Industrial Waste Management

TRI-S Environmental provides turnkey solutions for the identification, transportation, and disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste streams. We specialize in helping facilities remain in full compliance with state and federal EPA regulations while minimizing disposal costs.

Specialized Waste Streams Managed:

  • Flammable Solvents & Lab Reagents
  • Contaminated Soils (PCB, Lead, Petroleum)
  • Plating Sludges & Corrosive Liquids
  • Universal Waste (Batteries, Bulbs, Ballasts)
  • Non-Hazardous Industrial Byproducts

24/7 Emergency Spill Response

When an environmental incident occurs, speed is critical to containing liability. Our OSHA HAZWOPER certified emergency response teams are on call 24 hours a day to manage oil, fuel, and chemical spills on land or water.

Our Response Capabilities Include:

  • Over-the-road truck spills
  • Hydraulic line failures
  • Facility tank overfills
  • Sorbent boom deployment
  • Contaminated debris removal
  • Site restoration & testing

Tank Cleaning, Removal & Disposal

We provide comprehensive maintenance and closure services for both Underground Storage Tanks (UST) and Aboveground Storage Tanks (AST). Whether you are upgrading your facility or managing a property transfer, TRI-S ensures the process is safe and documented.

  • Confined space entry for internal tank cleaning
  • Removal and disposal of residual sludge and liquids
  • Full tank decommissioning and structural removal
  • Post-removal soil sampling and reporting

Lab-Pack Services

The disposal of small-quantity, diverse chemicals requires high technical expertise. Our chemists and technicians categorize, package, and manifest expired or unneeded chemicals for schools, hospitals, and industrial laboratories, ensuring every item is handled according to DOT and EPA protocols.
