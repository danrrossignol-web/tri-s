Environmental Services FAQ

How do I know if my industrial waste is classified as hazardous?

Waste is classified as hazardous if it is ignitable, corrosive, reactive, or toxic. TRI-S Environmental provides waste profiling and chemical characterization to ensure your facility remains in compliance with CT DEEP and EPA regulations.

What is your emergency spill response time in CT and MA?

We provide 24/7 rapid mobilization. Based in Ellington, CT, we have immediate access to major corridors like I-84 and I-91, allowing us to reach most sites in Connecticut and Western Massachusetts within hours.

Do you provide legal documentation for waste disposal?

Absolutely. We provide a full audit trail, including Uniform Hazardous Waste Manifests and Certificates of Disposal, ensuring you meet all state and federal record-keeping requirements.
