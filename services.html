Hazardous & Industrial Waste Management

TRI-S Environmental provides turnkey solutions for the identification, transportation, and disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste streams. We specialize in helping facilities remain in full compliance with state and federal EPA regulations.

Specialized Waste Managed:

  • Flammable Solvents & Lab Reagents
  • Contaminated Soils (PCB, Lead, Petroleum)
  • Plating Sludges & Corrosive Liquids
  • Universal Waste & Industrial Byproducts

Emergency Spill Response

When an environmental incident occurs, speed is critical. Our OSHA HAZWOPER certified emergency response teams are on call 24 hours a day to manage oil, fuel, and chemical spills on land or water.

Our Response Capabilities:

  • Over-the-road truck spills & Hydraulic failures
  • Facility tank overfills & Sorbent boom deployment
  • Contaminated debris removal
  • Site restoration & testing

Tank Cleaning, Removal & Disposal

We provide comprehensive maintenance and closure services for both Underground Storage Tanks (UST) and Aboveground Storage Tanks (AST).

  • Confined space entry for cleaning
  • Sludge and liquid removal
  • Full decommissioning & structural removal
  • Post-removal soil sampling

Lab-Pack Services

The disposal of small-quantity, diverse chemicals requires high technical expertise. Our chemists categorize, package, and manifest expired chemicals for schools, hospitals, and laboratories, ensuring DOT and EPA protocol compliance.

Transportation & Container Logistics

Via our sister company, Tri-S Trucking, we offer a dedicated fleet of licensed hazardous waste haulers. We provide the infrastructure to store waste safely onsite.

Container Provisioning: Drums, CBYs, Totes, and Bulk storage solutions.
